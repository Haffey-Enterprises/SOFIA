## Decision

The feedback loop and the Operational-plane distillation lifecycle are governed by the policy fixed here, instantiating ADR-008's per-source-class accountability controls (Architecture Decision Record 008 — Ground-Truth Mutation Governance, the upstream authority this record consumes). Decomposed into testable sub-decisions:

- **Decision.1 — Governance frame.** DDR-003 owns the governance policy for two of ADR-008 §2.2's controls: the **promotion class** (the per-write Executive Architect (EA) gate) and the **operational-distillation class** (the substantive, evidence-visible human review of the distinct distilled judgment). The two are a **two-distinct-controls seam**: deriving a distilled lesson into the Operational plane is governed by the distillation review; promoting knowledge into the authoritative selection planes (Catalog / Standards) is governed by the EA gate. Two controls, two failure modes (unsound judgment from noisy signal; unaccountable consolidation) — never one gate, never zero.
- **Decision.2 — Diagnosis policy.** The EA diagnoses each promotion candidate across **five named dimensions** (§2.2) to separate genuine knowledge from data-defect, transient-workaround, or spurious-artifact recurrence. Each candidate receives a **per-dimension verdict, recorded durably** — including inside a batch decision. **Weights and decision rules are EA judgment plus operational calibration, not ruled here** (§2.2).
- **Decision.3 — Roles.** Two distinct governance roles: the **promotion-gate EA role** (per-write accountability for promotion and un-promotion) and the **distillation-reviewer role** (per-distinct-lesson accountability for Operational-plane entry and lifecycle transitions). The EA holds both roles initially; the holder set of each role is a role-assignment act under the platform's access model (§1.3), not an amendment to this record — save that a non-human role-holder is an amendment question for ADR-008 §2.2, which fixes both controls as human review, not an assignment act here (§1.3).
- **Decision.4 — Signals and detection.** The signal taxonomy is organized by `PROPOSED_FROM` source and action path (§3.1), with **two action paths and a no-leak invariant** between them (§3.2). Detection de-duplicates by target key and against terminally-declined candidates, with two named lift paths (§3.3). **Detection is deterministic encoded logic over captured state; diagnosis is human judgment** (§3.4).
- **Decision.5 — Calibration-parameter mechanism.** One EA-owned, data-configured, audit-logged, fresh-read parameter mechanism governs every tunable value of the loop (§4); the parameter store homes with the detection-promotion mechanism that consumes it (§4.4). A **binding retention≥lookback coherence rule** is validated at configuration time — incoherent {window, lookback} pairs are rejected, not recorded (§4.3). **Every value is deferred to operational-onboarding calibration; this record asserts none.**
- **Decision.6 — Distillation governance.** A distilled lesson is a **candidate** until the distillation-reviewer approves it — excluded from ground-truth traversal throughout candidacy, carrying its evidence, with terminal-and-explainable rejection (§5.1). Lifecycle transitions of a distilled lesson (resolve, supersede, re-activate) are distillation judgments under the same review (§5.2). A **revalidation obligation** surfaces stale-but-active lessons for governed transition; the mechanism surfaces and never transitions (§5.3). Terminal-state archival is retention-tier demotion after a configured dwell, reversible (§5.4). Until the candidate shape and its review surface land, **no distillation write is conforming** — the stated interim (§5.1).
- **Decision.7 — Remedy boundary.** A promoted fact found wrong is remediated on a **replaceability-first** test: **supersession** (the default) when a correct replacement exists; **retraction** (the exception) only when the fact should never have been promoted **and** no correct replacement exists — the determination records durably a **defect-at-promotion versus world-moved** classification that informs the record but never decides the remedy (§6.1). **Re-admission of ground truth is always a forward, EA-gated act** — a governing-verdict flip on an executed retraction changes consumption nothing; un-retraction executes as re-promotion, and the standing detection analogue for executed retractions is ruled deferred-with-trigger (§6.2, §9).
- **Decision.8 — Conditional-scope governance.** An EA re-scope of a conditional fact carries a **deliberation obligation** with recorded basis (§6.3). Multi-condition composition is governed by the safe interim posture: **surface-to-EA as a scope-conflict, never silent auto-composition** (§6.3).
- **Decision.9 — Cross-class conflict resolution.** A gateway-detected cross-class scope conflict is resolved by the EA on a **four-outcome disposition space** — re-scope, remedy the promoted fact, uphold-with-recorded-rationale, hold-for-investigation — with standing conflicts surfaced every detection cycle until ruled (§6.4).
- **Decision.10 — Retention and audit.** Reasoning-Graph retention is a **role-based class model — three RG retention classes plus the Operational archival posture** (§7.1) — with two interim survival exemptions (decision-consumed Evidence; promotion-consumed substrate) held until their deferred survival mechanism lands, and the retrieval-affordance gap carried (§7.2, §9). The authoritative promotion-audit record is **graph-native and atomic with the governed act**; workflow stores are operational-secondary (§7.3).

## Rationale

The loop this record governs has never run. The governing tension is that authoring rich policy against zero traffic over-specifies, while leaving the EA gate and the distillation review ungoverned leaves ADR-008's accountability controls without the policy layer they route here. The cut applied throughout is the ratified mechanism-now/values-at-onboarding discipline: everything reversibility-expensive or upstream-anchored — the two-controls seam, the diagnosis dimensions, the role split, the no-leak invariant, the remedy boundary, the retention-class model, the audit posture — is ruled now; every calibration value (thresholds, windows, cadence, dwell, horizons) is structured now and parameterized at operational onboarding; everything with no driving instance is a named gap with a reversal path (§9). The empirical floor is stated plainly: n=0 promotions, n=0 distillations, n=0 retractions. Form is fixed; constants are held.

The design is deliberation-grounded: a seven-fork design deliberation ratified the substrate, and a second full deliberation re-validated every fork and disposition against the then-landed canon — ADR-008's acceptance, the graph schema's evolved constraint set, and the knowledge-service design — converting commitments the schema had since realized into citations and re-stating the one premise ADR-008 superseded (a formerly ungated distillation write path is now the reviewed distillation class). Both deliberations were ratified per item; the change log carries the session provenance.

## 1. Governance frame, authority, and roles

**1.1 Authority chain.** ADR-008 fixes the invariant (every mutation of enterprise ground truth carries a human-accountable control with a named answerable human, §2.1), the per-class control-types (§2.2), the un-promotion authority (§2.3), the withdrawal/expiry posture (§2.4), cross-class precedence (§2.5), and the governance boundary that routes remedy-boundary and all governance policy to this record (§2.6). DDR-003 is the downstream consumer authoring that policy. ADR-001 §2.5 is the reasoning-consolidation sub-case of the promotion class, cited through ADR-008. The promotion data-path (detect → propose → gate → materialize) and the three feedback-loop write authorities are DDR-001's (its Feedback-Loop Architecture section and Decision.7); the schema shapes are DDR-002's (§5); the gateway operations are the knowledge-service design's (SDD-001 §3.5). This record authors none of those — it points.

**1.2 The two-distinct-controls seam.** *Derive:* the distillation step writing a distilled lesson into the Operational plane is governed by the **distillation-class review** (ADR-008 §2.2's operational-distillation control) — substantive, evidence-visible, bounded by distinctness (per distinct lesson, never per observation; the raw signal stays in the external observability system of record, DDR-002 §2.3). *Promote:* entry into authoritative selection knowledge is governed by the **per-write EA gate** (DDR-002 §2.4/§5; the governing-verdict promoted-origin invariant, DDR-002 §7 #15). The seam is an authority boundary between two differently-calibrated human controls, not a gated/ungated split; a distillation-review approval makes a lesson consumable *track-record* and nothing more — Catalog/Standards entry still requires the EA gate.

**1.3 Roles.** The **promotion-gate EA role** discharges the promotion class's per-write accountability, in both directions (promotion and un-promotion). The **distillation-reviewer role** discharges the distillation class's per-distinct-lesson accountability: Operational-plane entry (§5.1) and lifecycle transitions (§5.2). The roles are distinct because the controls are distinct; the EA holds both initially, and each role's holder set is widened or reassigned by a role-assignment act — role and actor sourcing is the governance-state-manager service design's (forthcoming), per DDR-002 §2.4's routing. ADR-008 §2.2 fixes both controls as *human* review with a named human answerable; any future non-human role-holder is therefore an amendment question for that upstream authority, not an assignment act under this record. **Evidence-visibility is a validity precondition of the distillation review** (§5.1): a review conducted without traversable access to the underlying signal is not a conforming distillation-class review. The distillation-reviewer role is not a per-observation gate and its approval is not the promotion gate (§1.2). The broader non-reviewer permission surface (view/defer/queue rights) is a routed access-model gap (§9).

## 2. The promotion gate — diagnosis, batch, decline

**2.1 Gate shape (cited, not re-authored).** The gate's mechanics are landed contract: the mandatory, non-bypassable EA gate on the promotion path (DDR-001 Feedback-Loop Architecture; proposal-visibility, DDR-002 §7 #9), the per-candidate verdict on the `DECIDED_ON {outcome}` edge with strict `decided_at` monotonicity (DDR-002 §2.4, §7 #15), and the gateway's governing-verdict verification in the materialization transaction (SDD-001 §3.5.3). The proposal **carries the diagnosis substrate** the EA needs — supporting signal instances, recurrence count, the five-dimension inputs — so the gate is exercisable without side-channel evidence assembly.

**2.2 Diagnosis policy.** The EA diagnoses each candidate to separate **genuine knowledge** from **data-defect, transient-workaround, or spurious-artifact** recurrence. Dispositions map to the `DECIDED_ON` outcomes (`approved` / `approved_conditional` / `rejected`) and the three correction flavors (Refine / Request-new / Correct-scope, DDR-002 §5's generalized correction mechanism). A decision weighs **five diagnosis dimensions**:

1. **Recurrence strength** — how strongly the signal recurs.
2. **Evidence quality / source authority** — the confidence and authoritativeness of the supporting evidence.
3. **Genuine-pattern-vs-data-defect** — whether the recurrence reflects real knowledge or an upstream defect.
4. **Target deprecation / staleness state** — whether the knowledge target is current.
5. **Conditional-vs-unconditional applicability** — whether the knowledge is universally safe or scope-bounded; this dimension drives `approved_conditional` (§6.3).

**Per-dimension recording:** each candidate's decision records a verdict on each dimension, durably, as part of the decision act (§7.3). The recording requirement binds on content; a structured queryable capture shape is a named gap realized by a future additive graph-schema amendment at first real use (§9). **Weights and decision rules are EA judgment plus operational calibration — this record rules which dimensions a decision must weigh, not how to score them.**

**2.3 Batch eligibility.** Batching is permitted (the 1:many `DECIDED_ON` shape, DDR-002 §2.4). Every candidate in a batch retains its **own diagnosis** (the five dimensions) and its **own per-candidate verdict**: batching bundles the recording act, never the diagnosis. Volume-driven guardrails (size caps, queue-flood guards) are calibration gaps (§9).

**2.4 Decline semantics.** A rejected verdict is **terminal and durably explained** (the append-only terminal `status` with reason, DDR-002 §5), so a declined gap does not re-surface unexamined and re-burn EA attention. Detection honors this at §3.3.

## 3. Signals and detection

**3.1 Signal taxonomy.** Organized by `PROPOSED_FROM` source and action path; closed-but-additively-extensible; detection mechanics are the detection-promotion service design's (forthcoming):

- **Reasoning-Graph (RG) reasoning-recurrence → promotion path:** gap-recurrence (recurring gap conclusions) and override-recurrence (recurring human override, the primary feedback input per DDR-001's signal classes).
- **Operational track-record → promotion path:** distilled-lesson strength/weakness trends.
- **RG evidence-quality → source-quality path (not promotion):** low-confidence Evidence under overridden conclusions.
- **The Knowledge Graph (KG) participates as ground-truth-checked-against:** detection never re-proposes already-promoted knowledge.

**3.2 Action-class routing — the no-leak invariant.** Two action paths: **Path 1 — EA-gated knowledge promotion** (recurrence signals → candidate → EA gate → possible materialization); **Path 2 — source-quality feedback** (evidence-quality signals → ingestion-scoring / data-quality signal; **no EA gate, no KG mutation**). **No-leak invariant:** a source-quality signal never crosses into promotion except by independently re-entering as a promotion-eligible signal. A novel action class registers additively into one of the two paths.

**3.3 De-duplication.** Detection aggregates by **target key**: one live candidate per (target, proposal kind) — a recurring gap strengthens its candidate's substrate rather than spawning siblings. Detection also de-duplicates **against terminally-declined candidates** (§2.4): a signal matching a rejected candidate does not re-propose. Two lift paths: **EA-initiated re-open**, and **auto-re-open on material change** in the signal basis — the materiality threshold is a calibration parameter (§4).

**3.4 Determinism locus.** Signal detection is deterministic encoded logic over captured graph state; the diagnosis (§2.2) and every disposition in §5–§6 are human judgment. Nothing in this record delegates a governed judgment to the detection mechanism.

## 4. The calibration-parameter mechanism

One mechanism governs every tunable value of the loop. Parameters are **data-configured** (not code), **EA-owned** (only the promotion-gate EA role changes them), **audit-logged on every change** (§4.2), and **read fresh per run** (a change takes effect next run, no redeploy). The parameter set: **detection threshold** (minimum qualifying instances to surface a candidate) · **lookback window** (rolling counting period) · **run cadence** (detection and surfacing schedule) · **staleness horizon** (§5.3, with per-`pattern_type` override affordance) · **materiality threshold** (§3.3's auto-re-open) · **archival dwell** (§5.4) · **the working-tier retention-window set** (one window per RG role within §7.1's class 3). **Every value is an operational-onboarding calibration gap; this record asserts none.**

**4.1 Two-tier governance (adopted by pointer).** The mechanism adopts the two-tier calibration governance the inherited-confidence derivation ruling fixes (DDR-004 §6): config tuning is the operational, reversible experiment surface; a tuned value becomes the ratified default only by amendment, with the calibration evidence presented and ruled. Until amended, any override is a live experiment.

**4.2 Change-log integrity.** Every parameter change is durably recorded — actor, timestamp, prior value, new value, rationale. This is what discharges ADR-008 §2.1's *per-policy* accountability: a named human answerable for a governing policy requires the policy's change history to exist.

**4.3 Coherence validation — retention ≥ lookback.** Every §7.1 working-tier retention window must be **greater than or equal to** every detection lookback window that reads the retained data. A lookback reaching past retained data under-counts recurrence silently — detection degradation with no error surface. The mechanism validates this at configuration time; an incoherent {window, lookback} pair is rejected, not recorded.

**4.4 Placement.** The parameter store homes with the **detection-promotion mechanism** (the component that consumes it) — ownership follows consumption; EA authority and audit ride the mechanism wherever it is realized.

## 5. Distillation governance — the Operational-plane lifecycle

**5.1 Candidate stage.** A distilled lesson is a **candidate** until the distillation-reviewer approves it. Four requirements bind: (1) the candidate is **excluded from ground-truth traversal** for its entire candidacy — the same read-discipline species as proposal-visibility (DDR-002 §7 #9); (2) the review disposition space is **approve** (the lesson becomes consumable Operational track-record) or **reject — terminal and explainable**, so the same noisy signal does not re-enter unexamined; (3) the candidate **carries its evidence** — signal summary and `source_record_ref` to the external system of record — because evidence-visibility is the review's validity precondition (§1.3); (4) approval is the distillation-class review act (§1.2), accountable per-distinct-lesson. The candidate's structural realization (a candidate node class versus a status value) is a schema-layer choice owned by a future additive graph-schema amendment (§9); this record binds the requirements the shape must satisfy. **Interim posture: until that shape and the review surface land, no distillation write into the Operational plane is conforming.** The graph schema defines the `ObservedPattern` write shape and this record leaves it untouched (DDR-002 §2.3, which routes the checkpoint posture to ADR-008); what does not yet exist is a *governance-conforming use* of that shape — a distillation writer designed before the review machinery lands fails ADR-008 §6's accountability check at design review. Nothing is forfeited: no distillation process is built and no distillation traffic runs.

**5.2 Lifecycle transitions are distillation judgments.** Resolving, superseding, or re-activating a distilled lesson is itself a distillation judgment under the same distillation-class review (ADR-008 §2.4, which fixes a lesson's governed transition — a resolution or supersession — as itself a distillation judgment under §2.2; §1.2), ruled by the distillation-reviewer role. No lifecycle transition of a distilled lesson is a silent mechanical act.

**5.3 Revalidation.** The Operational plane does not age (its confidence basis is native lesson-reliability, DDR-002 §2.3), so a lesson whose underlying reality has changed never self-corrects; ADR-008 §2.4 commits the revalidation obligation and routes its cadence and trigger here. **Trigger:** an `active` lesson whose `last_observed_at` exceeds the **staleness horizon** (§4) is surfaced for revalidation. **The mechanism surfaces; it never transitions.** The disposition space is: **re-affirm** (still sound; a recorded review outcome, not a dismissal — the horizon resets), **resolve**, **supersede**, or **archive-track** — each a §5.2 reviewed act. Auto-archival on staleness is prohibited: staleness is signal; the human judgment is the control. The revalidation sweep rides the loop's scheduled cadence (§4); its mechanics are the detection-promotion service design's (forthcoming).

**5.4 Archival.** A lesson in terminal state (`resolved` / `superseded`) transitions to `archived` after a configured **dwell** (§4) — retention-tier demotion, never hard-deletion (ADR-008 §2.4; the `archived` status semantics, DDR-002 §2.3). Archival after a reviewed terminal transition is a policy-scheduled act under per-policy accountability (the judgment was already reviewed at §5.2); **re-activation of an archived lesson is a §5.2 distillation judgment**, which is what keeps the demotion reversible and governed in both directions.

## 6. Remedies and conflict resolution

**6.1 The remedy boundary.** A promoted fact found wrong is remediated on a **replaceability-first, conjunctive-retraction** test. **Supersession is the default:** if a correct replacement exists, the remedy is supersession — even when the original promotion was defective; the defect is recorded in the remedy rationale and lineage is preserved as a superseded version. **Retraction is the exception**, requiring both prongs: the fact **should never have been promoted** *and* **no correct replacement exists** — reserved for the case where leaving any successor would itself be wrong. The determination records, durably: the remedy chosen, the replaceability finding, a **defect-at-promotion versus world-moved** classification (a diagnostic axis that informs the record and the §2.2 dimensions' calibration; it never decides the remedy), and — for retraction — why no correct replacement exists. The diagnoser is the promotion-gate EA, per-write: both remedies execute as EA-gated acts on landed mechanics (atomic supersession under the conditional-scope carry-forward invariant, DDR-002 §7 #22; the EA-gated reversing candidate under the retraction-gated invariant, DDR-002 §7 #21; SDD-001 §3.5.3–§3.5.4).

**6.2 Re-admission is a forward act.** Read-exclusion of a retracted fact keys on the executed `RETRACTS` edge exactly as landed (DDR-002 §5; the edge's writing is the materialization act under the executed-proposal scoping of the `proposal_kind`↔`RETRACTS` consistency invariant, DDR-002 §7 #25). A later append-only re-decision that flips the executed retraction's governing verdict **records** that the retraction is contested and **changes consumption nothing** — re-entry of a fact into authoritative ground truth is never a ledger side-effect. **Un-retraction executes as re-promotion:** a fresh forward candidate through the full EA gate, materializing a new active version via the supersession path (DDR-002 §6) — the replaceability-first test applied to the retraction itself (the valid fact is its own correct replacement). A standing detection analogue for executed retractions whose governing verdict is non-approving — the retraction-side mirror of the promoted-origin invariant (DDR-002 §7 #15) — is a named gap (§9); the forward-act rule is what makes that gap an audit-reconciliation concern rather than a consumption defect.

**6.3 Conditional-scope governance.** Scope-setting is an EA accountability: the `Condition` predicate defines where promoted knowledge is safe to reuse — too broad over-admits (the failure the conditional-consumption invariant, DDR-002 §7 #19, guards), too narrow strands genuine knowledge — so the predicate is EA-authored in the approval act and audit-recorded as diagnosis basis. **Re-scope deliberation obligation:** the gateway enforces the *presence and structural consistency* of a supersession's scope disposition (the carry-forward gate, DDR-002 §7 #22; SDD-001 §3.5); that the EA *deliberated* the re-scope is this record's obligation — a re-scope decision records what changed in the fact or its context to justify widening, narrowing, or lifting the scope. **Multi-condition interim posture:** the multi-`HAS_CONDITION` consumption combinator and condition-set lifecycle remain the graph schema's named gap; until resolved, a further conditional promotion of an already-conditional fact **surfaces to the EA as a scope-conflict requiring explicit diagnosis — never silent auto-composition** — preserving the never-consumed-out-of-scope guarantee through the unresolved case.

**6.4 Cross-class scope-conflict resolution.** ADR-008 §2.5 fixes withhold-and-escalate with gateway-mechanical detection; the knowledge-service's cross-origin block (SDD-001 §3.5) is the authorized instance. This record fixes the resolution layer. **Resolver: the promotion-gate EA** — the conflict is definitionally a collision with an EA-set scope, and the scope-setter resolves. **Disposition space, four outcomes:** (1) **re-scope** the conditional fact (an EA re-scoping decision under §6.3; the blocked write then retries clean); (2) **remedy the promoted fact** (the conflict reveals the promoted fact is wrong — §6.1 applies; ingested reality then lands); (3) **uphold the scope** — the EA-set scope governs and the colliding write stays excluded, recorded as a ruling with rationale, because "which reality governs" is a question ADR-008 requires a human to answer on the record; (4) **hold-for-investigation** — explicitly non-terminal, named owner, converted to (1)–(3). **Standing-conflict visibility:** an unresolved conflict means the platform knowingly holds observed reality out of the KG — the fail-safe direction, but only while visible: open conflicts surface every detection cycle (§4) until ruled; no auto-timeout, no auto-precedence. **Detection stays structural:** the gateway detects structural collisions only; this record commissions no semantic conflict detection — scope-adjacent conflicts invisible to structural detection reach human judgment through the §5.3 revalidation sweep and the §5.1/§5.2 reviews, and that coverage boundary is stated rather than papered over.

## 7. Retention and audit

**7.1 Retention classes (Reasoning Graph, role-based, non-uniform).** (1) **Durable governance ledger** — promotion candidates, promotion decisions and their edges, and provenance summaries: never expires; this is the accountability record. (2) **Decision-consumed Evidence** — Evidence in the `SUPPORTED_BY` closure of the reasoning trail of a session that `PRODUCED` a Solution which reached the selection-constituting `approved` gate outcome (DDR-002 §5): survival-exempt per §7.2. (3) **Working reasoning tier** — sessions, conclusions, Evidence, and rejected alternatives outside classes 1–2: bounded **per-role retention windows** (one per RG role), values deferred (§4), each coherence-validated against lookbacks (§4.3). (4) **Operational track-record** — not an RG class; its posture is §5.4's archival demotion. Whether non-selected candidates' reasoning trails warrant class-2 survival resolves with the survival-mechanism gap (§9).

**7.2 Survival exemptions.** *Decision-consumed Evidence:* ADR-008 §2.4 commits that a decision's traceable rationale survives the mutation of what it consumed, and its §5.3 names the gap — the frozen-provenance guarantee is promotion-scoped (the at-promotion `ProvenanceSummary` materialization invariant, DDR-002 §7 #20), so a decision that consumed a fact *without* promoting it rests on bounded RG retention. **Interim: class-2 Evidence is exempt from expiry** until a survival mechanism for decision-consumed Evidence lands (a graph-schema question, deferred by name, §9). At zero traffic the exemption forfeits nothing and is reversible. *Promotion-consumed substrate:* every `PROPOSED_FROM` target of a durable terminal-`promoted` promotion-kind candidate is **retention-exempt** — the provenance chain (DDR-002 §5) stays a live traversal for basis nodes the Evidence-scoped provenance summary does not freeze (a rejected-alternative override basis, a conclusion node). The exempt set is small by construction: promotions are EA-gated and low-volume.

**7.3 Audit posture — graph-native, atomic, audit-first.** The authoritative audit record of every governed act in this record — promotion, retraction, re-scope, conflict resolution, distillation review, revalidation disposition — is the **graph-native governance record, written atomically with the act** (the Governance plane's immutable append-only posture, DDR-002 §2.4; the single-transaction materialization shape, SDD-001 §3.5.3). Workflow stores hold operational-secondary state (queues, in-flight review state) and are never the audit source of truth. This is ADR-008 §3's answerable-by-traversal made operational: the record of *who ruled what, on which candidate, on what basis* is reachable by traversal from the governed fact itself. For §2.2's per-dimension verdicts that reachability is earned in stages: the decision act and its durable rationale are graph-native now; per-dimension *structured* traversal arrives with the structured-capture gap (§9), and until it lands the verdicts ride the decision's recorded rationale. The audit content requirements bind on the decision act; capture-shape mechanics beyond landed schema are the named structured-capture gap (§9).

## 8. Conformance

Verified at three-hat (LAA / SA / EA — Lead Application Architect / Solution Architect / Enterprise Architect) design review for any design touching the feedback loop or the Operational plane, and — where mechanized — by the named enforcement locus. Tiering follows the graph schema's convention: **safety-relevant** invariants guard ground-truth integrity; **follow** invariants guard audit/consumption quality. Enforcement state is marked honestly; an unmarked mechanization claim is a defect.

1. **Non-bypassable gates** *(safety-relevant; mechanized via the DDR-002 §7 safety-critical-tier checks — harness Increment 1: graph-state assertions enforced, gateway-behavioral contracts specified with required-flip at the gateway build; DDR-001 conformance check 4 aspirational, mechanization deferred per its own header)* — promotion and un-promotion ride the landed gate invariants (DDR-002 §7 #9, #15, #21, #22; DDR-001 conformance check 4); this record adds no bypass and no design may introduce one.
2. **Distillation-class review before consumable track-record** *(safety-relevant; design-review-enforced, interim)* — no distillation write is conforming until the candidate shape and review surface land (§5.1); enforced at ADR-008 §6's review checkpoint pending mechanization in the detection-promotion service design (forthcoming).
3. **Surface-never-transition** *(safety-relevant; aspirational → detection-promotion service design)* — no revalidation or staleness mechanism auto-transitions a lesson (§5.3).
4. **Re-admission forward-act-only** *(safety-relevant; holds by construction on the read path)* — landed read-discipline keys exclusion on the executed `RETRACTS` edge and no consumption path evaluates governing verdicts at read time (§6.2); by-construction is a read-path claim, not an audit-completeness claim — audit-side detection of a contested executed retraction is the named gap (§9).
5. **No-leak invariant** *(safety-relevant; design-review-enforced, interim — no detection mechanism exists to leak through until the detection-promotion service design builds one, and that design fails review if it admits a crossing path; the mechanized locus arrives with it)* — no source-quality signal crosses into promotion (§3.2).
6. **Per-dimension diagnosis recorded** *(follow; design-review-enforced, interim)* — every promotion decision records five per-dimension verdicts per candidate (§2.2); mechanized capture arrives with the structured-capture gap (§9).
7. **Decline-terminal + detection de-dup** *(follow; terminal status mechanized in landed schema; detection-side de-dup aspirational → detection-promotion service design)* — §2.4, §3.3.
