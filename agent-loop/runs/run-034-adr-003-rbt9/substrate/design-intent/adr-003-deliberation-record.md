## The counter-framing (operator, session-opening — premise-level applied learning)

Tad rejected re-anchoring on the legacy hub-and-spoke/Pub-Sub pattern: those early decisions became too durable ("inertia"), and his thinking has evolved faster than delivery — the spine is now **agent-to-agent, orchestrated loops, enterprise standards in a graph database for constraints**, with the agent-loop construct built deliberately to learn/set that spine and HEX as the drawn experience. Core requirements stated: the architecture must never preclude agent-to-agent communication (use cases: Echo→Sage signal routing; Iris↔Sage mid-work consult); AOE central early, decreasing over time; corporate gates/checkpoints manifest (largely via HEX) but the process is non-deterministic — not process automation.

**Analytical keystone (ratified within Item 2):** the legacy design banned agent-to-agent communication because topology was its only governance mechanism; the corpus has since anchored governance to the graph substrate (sole-owner gateway, named write authority, graph-captured gates, capture invariant, constraints-from-KG). Once the control surface is the graph, routing topology stops being load-bearing for governability — route-agnostic invariants, route-free evolution.

## Ratified items (all 2026-07-20, per item)

**Item 1 — Doctype/number gate.** ADR, number 003, `docs/adr/ADR-003-platform-patterns.md`. Deletion test passes on the content class; number reserved by the RBT-59 standing ladder and unused. Post-em-dash working title explicitly discarded as anchor at ratification.

**Item 2 — The orchestration model (the spine; RATIFIED with enthusiasm).** ADR-003 commits four things: (1) work performed in **orchestrated runs** — non-deterministic composition, AOE-coordinated, multi-origin initiation (human brief via the HEX surface class, scheduled, agent-observed signal); (2) **specialized agents as the platform's units of work**, collaborating semantically; agent-to-agent communication is first-class and never precluded; (3) **governance anchors to the graph, not the topology** — capture, constraint-binding, gateway-mediated graph access, and checkpoint gates are route-agnostic invariants; (4) **coordination trajectory** — AOE-coordinated operating regime now, direct interaction growing as learning accumulates, invariants holding identically at every point (the ADR-001 §2.1 regime+trajectory device, applied to coordination). Deferral set: transport/protocol (per interaction, SDD-time), bus product, static call-graph, roster enumeration, and any permission machinery for opening direct routes (under-govern-until-it-hurts). Floors: orchestrated-loop model proven in one domain (33 agent-loop runs, document review); SOFIA-runtime instantiation n=0; HEX is experience intent, not service architecture; trajectory far end directional, not scheduled.

**Item 3 — Run anatomy.** Four properties committed — three by citation, one new: (1) every run leaves a durable, traversable record (run = `ReasoningSession`; ADR-001 §2.2 / ADR-002 §2.6 — cite); (2) human-accountable gates at checkpoints (ADR-008 family — cite; route-agnostic); (3) versioned substrate (DDR-001 version-pinning — cite; the hash-pinned-substrate lesson); (4) **isolation as a first-class instrument property** (new ruling — stance-isolation generalized; orchestration cannot assume all agents in a run share full context). Plus the **honest-halt posture**: non-convergence is an honest outcome and a product. Ratified rationale enrichment: tuning/data/learning raise convergence rates, but every new tech/pattern/data/solution reinjects unforeseeable variability — non-convergence is the *signal*, and a platform that treats it as failure will learn to suppress its own signal. Loop mechanics (passes, arbiter, ledger formats, halt taxonomies) stay below ADR altitude.

**Item 4 — Constraints-from-graph (named platform pattern; the commitment with no prior home).** Enterprise constraints (standards, directives, policy) bind agents **from the KG**: graph-resident (Standards plane, DDR-001's), consulted through the sole-owner gateway, version-pinned at consultation, evidence-linked in the RG (authority-citation discipline made structural). Binding is **invocation-agnostic** (same constraints regardless of who woke the agent) — what makes route freedom safe. Constraint evaluation is deterministic encoded reasoning (ADR-001 §2.3). The testable line: **"if it binds, it's in the graph; if it's in a prompt, it doesn't bind."** Not ruled: constraint schema/vocabulary (DDR-002), predicate semantics (→ constraint-validator design per DDR-003's routing), corpus content, prompt-carried task context. Floor: committed now, enforced in stages — Standards-plane ingestion (ADR-004 leg) and constraint-validator unauthored; earned tense mandatory.

**Item 5 — Component taxonomy (amended in deliberation to add recursion).** Four classes by the **authorship discriminator** (ADR-002 §2.6 + ADR-001 §2.2): **orchestrator** (AOE; HEX's core persona is its face) · **specialized agents** (reasoning-bearing; output captured as attributed reasoning) · **substrate services** (execute/enforce/store without authoring; knowledge-service the canonical instance, ai-gateway here too) · **surfaces** (humans brief/ratify/observe). What earns a new agent: distinct specialization with named reasoning authority; adding one is **additive** (no peer changes — the one legacy Decision-A clause that survives, generalized off the hub). **Zero roster enumeration**; canon-named instances only (AOE, ASA, knowledge-service). Orchestrator's own composition-decision capture: routed to the AOE SDD under ADR-001 §2.4. **Recursive-composition clause (operator-prompted amendment, sub-agents):** an agent may discharge its specialization by composing sub-agents (Tempest → functional/perf/UI testers); a sub-run is a run (Item 3 invariants at every level); every reasoning sub-agent's judgment is captured attributed to it at depth; the **output of record belongs to the composing agent** (accountability concentrates; provenance stays deep). No registration/permission/depth machinery; sub-agents doubly un-rostered. Scale-free insight carried by the recursion clause; the four classes retained (no collapse of orchestrator into "just another agent" — preserves ADR-002 §2.6 lifecycle clarity).
