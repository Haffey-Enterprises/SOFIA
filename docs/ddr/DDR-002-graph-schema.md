## Decision

DDR-002 fixes the **node / relationship / constraint / index contract** for the SOFIA graph system-of-record. It owns the schema; it does **not** own architecture (DDR-001), feedback-loop governance (DDR-003), or service realization (the SDDs). Decomposed into testable ruling components:

- **Decision.1** вҖ” The schema defines the node, relationship, constraint, and index contract for the **Knowledge Graph (KG)**, the **Reasoning Graph (RG)**, and the **Artifact node-family**, co-resident in one Neo4j Enterprise instance (DDR-001 Decision.1).
- **Decision.2** вҖ” The KG is the **five core planes** fixed by DDR-001 (Catalog, Environment, Operational, Governance, Standards) **plus the Extension plane**, where cost registers as the first Extension registration вҖ” cost is **not** a sixth core plane.
- **Decision.3** вҖ” The RG is **captured reasoning**, cross-graph-linked to the KG, and is **not** a KG plane (carries no plane label).
- **Decision.4** вҖ” The **Artifact family** (produced deliverables вҖ” solutions, BCDR (business-continuity / disaster-recovery) plans, build sheets) is a node-family *within* the two-graph store вҖ” **neither a KG plane nor a third graph** вҖ” joined to the KG and RG only by edges.
- **Decision.5** вҖ” The schema is bound by a **two-axis provenance model** (`origin_mechanism` Г— `derivation_class`), a **reversibility-tiered completeness posture**, DB-enforced uniqueness/existence constraints, and a **CI (continuous-integration)-only conformance-invariant set** with a bound, named exposure window.
- **Decision.6** вҖ” This DDR owns the schema contract only; architecture (DDR-001), feedback-loop governance (DDR-003), and service realization (the SDDs) are routed, not authored, here.
- **Decision.7** вҖ” Un-promotion of a promoted KG fact is the **retraction shape**: an Enterprise Architect (EA)-gated reversing `CandidatePromotion` (`proposal_kind: retraction`) + `RETRACTS` edge, through the same EA gate as a forward promotion (В§5; В§7 #21, #25).
- **Decision.8** вҖ” A promoted node's provenance survives `Evidence` retention via the **provenance-survival guarantee**: a `(:Reasoning:ProvenanceSummary)` materialized **at promotion**, complete against the В§5-defined originating-`Evidence` span (В§4/В§5; В§7 #20).
- **Decision.9** вҖ” Promoted knowledge carries the **`applicability_state` conditional-consumption model**: a `conditional` node is admitted only where the consuming context satisfies its `Condition`, with per-version supersession carry-forward (В§5; В§7 #19, #22).
- **Decision.10** вҖ” Versioned ground truth follows **Option A supersession** вҖ” retained version nodes + effective-dating + `superseded_by`, atomic re-bind (В§6).
- **Decision.11** вҖ” Confidence-derivation totality is a **per-class property established at the definition boundary**: every node-class declares exactly one confidence-derivation basis вҖ” in В§2.1вҖ“2.5 for core planes, in the `PlaneDefinition` registration contract for Extension planes (В§2.6) вҖ” so a well-formed citation either derives or rejects typed, and `CONFIDENCE_UNDERIVABLE` (the typed fail-closed rejection, В§2) is unreachable for any well-formed class (В§2; В§7 #26, #27). Derivation semantics and values are DDR-004's.

## Rationale

This ruling is **deliberation-grounded**: it is the output of the pre-authoring design session, which deliberated the node/edge/constraint shape plane by plane against DDR-001's ratified data architecture and ADR-001/ADR-002's reasoning-and-system-of-record commitments. The design space, the alternatives weighed at each plane, and the disposition of every review finding were worked through in that session; this DDR carries the settled contract.

The completeness posture is deliberately **reversibility-scoped, not a uniform dial**: identity keys, constraints, edge-binding properties, and invariant/traversal-bearing properties are locked now because they are expensive to reverse downstream (DDR-002 gates the downstream service SDDs and is cited by DDR-003); descriptive and rich application payload stay schema-on-write and SDD-level. Where the design cycle surfaced a mechanism with **no empirical instance yet** вҖ” and locking it would over-specify вҖ” the capability is named as a gap (see *Named Gaps*) and re-instated by additive amendment when a real instance arises, rather than carried speculatively. This is the empirical-warrant discipline that governs what is locked versus named.

## 1. Schema grammar & global conventions

**Naming.** Node labels `PascalCase`; relationship types `SCREAMING_SNAKE`; properties `snake_case`; primary keys `<entity>_id`.

**Provenance (origin-neutral group; two-axis).** Every **KG** node carries a provenance group вҖ” minimally `origin_mechanism` + `recorded_at` (and `source_record_ref` where applicable); DB-existence-constrained (В§7). Provenance is split into **two orthogonal axes**:

- **`origin_mechanism`** (how the node entered вҖ” `ingested` external ground truth В· `authored` human/SOFIA-authored В· `promoted` materialized through the feedback-loop EA gate В· `derived` produced by an internal process), always present and invariant-bearing;
- **`derivation_class`** (the nature of the content вҖ” `primary` В· `distilled` В· `aggregated`), present where it carries information.

The two axes form the **provenance scoping matrix**: `ingested вҶ’ primary` В· `authored вҶ’ primary` В· `derived вҶ’ distilled | aggregated` В· **`promoted вҶ’ N/A`**. `derivation_class` is **N/A for `promoted`** by design: a promoted node carries `origin_mechanism: promoted` as its provenance-axis fact, and its pre-promotion *content nature* (was the source distilled? aggregated? authored reasoning?) lives **at the source node**, recovered by traversing the provenance chain (В§5) вҖ” never denormalized onto the promoted node. This de-tangles a process-vs-nature conflation: a distilled `ObservedPattern` and a computed `CapabilityCostEstimate` each become independently queryable on both axes вҖ” (`origin_mechanism: derived`, `derivation_class: distilled`) and (`origin_mechanism: derived`, `derivation_class: aggregated`) respectively. **Matrix note:** a **SOFIA-recorded human decision** вҖ” an EA's approval act made in SOFIA, e.g. a `PromotionDecision` (В§2.4) вҖ” is the **`authored вҶ’ primary`** case: `authored` because SOFIA is the issuing authority (no external record to capture), `primary` because the decision content is original, neither distilled nor aggregated.

**`source_record_ref`** is the structured, durable reference to the originating external system of record вҖ” `{source_system_class, record_id}` (system named by *class*, not a vendor product, per DDR-001's source-named-by-class convention). The graph holds the reference, not the record: **required on `ingested`** and on **`derived` with `derivation_class: distilled`** (a distilled `ObservedPattern` references its telemetry source вҖ” aligns В§7 check #17); **permitted but not required on `aggregated`** `derived` nodes (which derive from internal version-pins, not an external record); **N/A on `authored`/`promoted`** (provenance is internal lineage).

**`recorded_at`** is the uniform system/record time (when the graph captured the node); domain timestamps (`decided_at`, `first_observed_at`/`last_observed_at`, `computed_at`, `generated_at`) are node-type-specific and never conflated with it.

The `promoted` vs. `ingested` distinction вҖ” a **DDR-001 conformance-check #6 obligation** (promoted knowledge must be distinguishable from ingested) вҖ” is carried by `origin_mechanism`. RG and Artifact provenance differ (В§4, В§5).

**Provenance scope.** The provenance *existence constraint* (В§7) scopes to: **KG nodes**, the **three authored RG types** (`ReasoningSession`, `ReasoningProgress`, `RejectedAlternative`, all `origin_mechanism: authored`), the **derived, group-carrying `ProvenanceSummary`** (`origin_mechanism: derived`; В§4), and the **Artifact family** (В§5). `RejectedAlternative` carries the group as authored content because DDR-001 frames it as ASA (Architecture Solutioning Agent)-authored and the *primary feedback-loop input* (В§Reasoning Graph) вҖ” the highest-leverage promotion signal must not carry the weakest provenance. **`Evidence` is the sole surrogate-only RG type:** its provenance is recovered structurally via `SOURCED_FROM` (the atomic capture-unit, В§7), not a carried group (В§4).

**No vectors вҖ” both halves.** *Negative half:* no node or edge carries vector/embedding properties (documented invariant + conformance check). *Positive half:* the graph-native retrieval substrate that *replaces* vector similarity is the existing structure вҖ” the `l1/l2/l3` capability/pattern taxonomy (В§2.1), the selection-edge web (В§3), and the track-record subgraph (В§2.3). DDR-002 **names** these as the no-vector substitute and restates the no-vector prohibition as binding; the concrete **retrieval affordance** (graph-traversal query patterns; whether a `SIMILAR_TO` edge is warranted and how it would be populated without embeddings) is **routed to the knowledge-service / solutioning SDDs**, not designed here.

**Classification / no-PHI.** Data classification is gateway-enforced at ingestion; SOFIA stores no PHI (protected-health-information) *data*. Note the distinction: PHI/PCI *policies* are legitimate Standards knowledge (rules about architectures that handle such data); classification strips the data, not the rules.

**Plane membership вҖ” hybrid (KG-scoped).** Every **KG** node carries a **secondary plane label** (enables cheap label-scoped traversal) **and** the plane is declared in a `PlaneDefinition` registry node (В§2.6). Core planes are fixed by DDR-001; the registry is the Extension surface. **RG nodes and Artifact-family nodes carry no KG plane label** вҖ” they are not plane citizens; they are reached by cross-graph traversal (В§5). This exemption is what lets plane-scoped ground-truth traversals skip reasoning state and produced artifacts structurally.

**Completeness posture вҖ” the tiering that governs every node/edge.** Scope by *reversibility*, not a uniform dial:

- **T1** вҖ” identity / structural keys, constraints, edge-binding properties. *Expensive to reverse вҶ’ complete now.*
- **T2** вҖ” invariant- or traversal-bearing properties (filters, discriminators, version pins, status). *Drives behavior вҶ’ complete now, indexed where it drives traversal.*
- **T3** вҖ” descriptive payload. *Cheap to add (schema-on-write) вҶ’ minimal now, gaps named not padded.*
- **T4** вҖ” rich application payload. *SDD-level, not schema.*

**Contested-T2 annotation.** Where a T2 value-set is **POC (proof-of-concept)-asserted** rather than invariant-grounded, it is flagged *(contested)* so a spike-generalization review pass can revisit it. Current contested T2s: `conclusion_type` enum (В§4), `GateDecision.gate` enum (В§2.4), `polarity` neutral option (В§2.3), `basis_strength` (В§5). Invariant-grounded T2s (plane labels, version pins, status discriminators) are not flagged.

**Denormalization boundary (consolidated).** Cross-plane concerns stay in their owning plane, reached by traversal вҖ” never duplicated onto a node. The "never duplicated" rule governs **hot-path live-fact duplication**; it is **not** breached by two bounded, justified kinds, named here once so neither reads as a local exception:

- **Point-in-time / retention-boundary snapshots** вҖ” `Evidence.fact_summary` + `source_node_version` pins (В§4), and the frozen provenance snapshot that preserves a promoted node's lineage past `Evidence` expiry (the `(:Reasoning:ProvenanceSummary)` node, В§4/В§5). These capture *what a fact said at a point in time*, precisely because the live source can drift or be deliberately retained-out вҖ” a **temporal-fidelity** concern the never-duplicated rule does not govern (its "traverse to the live home" presupposes a live home).
- **Sync-checked edge-mirrors** вҖ” `dependency_manifest` (`PolicyRule`, В§2.5) mirrors `GOVERNED_BY`/`MANDATES` structure to make opaque-logic reads introspectable, kept honest by В§7 conformance check #10 (the mirror must match the edges) and its declared-not-undeclared caveat (В§7). A `Condition`'s manifest (В§2.4) is declared-introspectable without an edge mirror вҖ” consumed at retrieval-time evaluation (В§7 #19, #10).

## 2. The planes вҖ” node schemas

Property tier in parentheses. `provenance (T1)` denotes the provenance group. В§2.1вҖ“В§2.5 are the five core planes fixed by DDR-001; В§2.6 is the Extension surface, where cost registers first.

**Confidence-derivation basis (per-class declaration).** Every node-class in В§2.1вҖ“В§2.6 declares exactly one confidence-derivation basis at its definition boundary вҖ” for core planes here in В§2.1вҖ“В§2.5, for Extension planes in the `PlaneDefinition` registration contract (В§2.6). The four bases: **`native_confidence`** (basis 1 вҖ” the node carries its own `confidence`; the citation inherits it) В· **`flat_base`** (basis 2 вҖ” non-aging; staleness governed by supersession/lifecycle, no decay) В· **`aging`** (basis 3 вҖ” class base Г— freshness decay over a declared domain freshness operand) В· **`non_citable`** (basis 4 вҖ” not a valid `Evidence` source; a citation rejects typed at capture, В§2.6). **Resolution rule:** a plane-level declaration governs every node-label in its section absent a per-label override; every node-label resolves exactly one basis. **The citable universe is exact:** `SOURCED_FROM` targets KG nodes only (В§5), and Reasoning and Artifact nodes carry no KG plane label вҖ” so the declarations in В§2.1вҖ“В§2.6 cover the citable universe completely, and `CONFIDENCE_UNDERIVABLE` is unreachable for any well-formed class (defense-in-depth only; DDR-004). **A declared basis governs the derivation when a node of that class is cited; it imposes no requirement that such citations exist вҖ” evidence completeness is never a derivation concern.** Declarations carry basis kind (and, for `aging`, the freshness operand) only вҖ” no values: derivation semantics, base values, and their invariant-vs-contested treatment are DDR-004's.

### 2.1 Catalog вҖ” the selection graph (versioned ground truth)

- **`(:Catalog:Pattern)`** вҖ” `pattern_id` (T1 PK) В· `version` (T1) В· `pattern_type` (T2, indexed) В· `status` (T2, indexed, versioned) В· `effective_from`/`superseded_by` (T2) В· `name` (T3) В· provenance (T1)
- **`(:Catalog:Capability)`** вҖ” `capability_id` (T1 PK) В· `version` (T1) В· `name` (T3) В· `l1_taxonomy`/`l2_taxonomy`/`l3_taxonomy` (T2) В· `status` (T2) В· `description` (T3) В· provenance (T1)
- **`(:Catalog:Technology)`** вҖ” `technology_id` (T1 PK) В· `version` (T1) В· `name` (T3) В· `vendor` (T3) В· `platform` (T3) В· `approval_status` (T2, indexed) В· `version_constraint`/`min`/`max` (T2) В· `deprecation_date` (T2) В· `tier_applicability[]` (T2) В· `approved_data_classifications[]` (T2) В· provenance (T1)
- **`(:Catalog:IacTemplate)`** вҖ” `iac_template_id` (T1 PK) В· `template_type` (T2) В· `name` (T3) В· `version` (T1) В· `status` (T2) В· provenance (T1)

*Confidence-derivation basis:* **`flat_base`** (basis 2), plane-level вҖ” versioned authoritative ground truth; staleness is supersession, not decay (В§2 preamble; DDR-004).

The `l1/l2/l3` taxonomy + the selection edges are the **graph-native retrieval substrate** (no-vector positive half, В§1) вҖ” affordance design вҶ’ knowledge-service / solutioning SDDs.

*Reference vs. produced.* "Solution documents" as *reference* Catalog content (template/exemplar solutions, library knowledge) are distinct from the **produced** `(:Artifact:Solution)` keystone (В§5). A reference solution is Catalog ground truth; a produced solution is an Artifact. They are not the same node, and Catalog never holds the produced ASD (Architecture Solution Document, В§5/DDR-001).

### 2.2 Environment вҖ” the as-running graph (ages via freshness)

- **`(:Environment:DeployedService)`** вҖ” `deployed_service_id` (T1 PK) В· `service_type` (T3) В· `lifecycle_state` (T2, indexed) В· `observed_at` (T2) В· provenance (T1, `origin_mechanism: ingested` В· `derivation_class: primary`, runtime/CMDB (configuration-management-database) source class)
- **`(:Environment:DeploymentEnvironment)`** вҖ” `environment_id` (T1 PK) В· `name` (T3) В· `environment_class` (T2, indexed вҖ” production/staging/dev) В· provenance (T1). ***Per-label basis override:* `flat_base` (basis 2)** вҖ” the plane's structurally-stable member: a realized environment *identity*, which does not age. Its absence of `observed_at` is **correct by design, not a schema gap** вҖ” a freshness field would model non-existent staleness, and the option "add `observed_at`" stands rejected (DDR-004).
- **`(:Environment:ConfigurationItem)`** вҖ” `ci_id` (T1 PK) В· `ci_type` (T2, indexed) В· `name` (T3) В· `observed_at` (T2) В· provenance (T1)

*Plane signature:* freshness (`observed_at`) вҖ” runtime facts are less authoritative than catalog ground truth; this lets the RG weight environment evidence lower. *Confidence-derivation basis:* **`aging`** (basis 3), plane-level, freshness operand `observed_at` вҖ” with the `DeploymentEnvironment` per-label override above (structural identity is the signature's one exception: it does not age). *Lifecycle distinction:* `DeploymentEnvironment` is the **realized** runtime environment, distinct from a solution's design-time `target_environment` (В§5). Environment is DDR-001-sanctioned CMDB-sourced ground truth.

### 2.3 Operational вҖ” the track-record graph (durable distillation, not telemetry)

- **`(:Operational:ObservedPattern)`** вҖ” `observed_pattern_id` (T1 PK) В· `polarity` (T2, indexed вҖ” `strength` / `weakness` / optional `neutral` *(contested)*) В· `pattern_type` (T2, indexed) В· `description` (T3) В· `observation_window` (T3) В· `confidence` (T2 вҖ” **lesson reliability**; see В§4 canonical def; per-target observation certainty rides the `OBSERVED_IN` edge, В§3) В· `first_observed_at`/`last_observed_at` (T2) В· `status` (T2, indexed вҖ” active/superseded/resolved/**archived**) В· provenance (T1, `origin_mechanism: derived` В· `derivation_class: distilled`)

*Confidence-derivation basis:* **`native_confidence`** (basis 1), plane-level вҖ” the plane's authority measure is the node's own `confidence` (lesson reliability); a citation inherits it.

The KG does **not** mirror transient SoR (system-of-record) telemetry. It holds the durable distilled lesson вҖ” a recurring operational pattern, of **dual polarity** (a clean track record is as informative as a weakness). **No in-graph TTL.** Raw telemetry stays TTL-bounded in the **external** observability/AIOps SoR; it is not a KG plane and is not mirrored. **Bounded by distinctness.** Distillation is **update-in-place** вҖ” the *active* `ObservedPattern` set is bounded by the number of distinct durable lessons (each updated as evidence accrues via `first_observed_at`/`last_observed_at`/`confidence`), **not** a node-per-observation; the never-delete posture is qualified to *never silently delete*, with terminal-state (`resolved`/`superseded`) patterns transitioning to **`archived`** per **DDR-003** policy. `archived` is **retention-tier demotion, never hard-deletion** вҖ” an archived pattern stays traceable and is reversible if a dormant pattern recurs. DDR-002 commits the `archived` `status` value; the archival **policy** (the terminal-state-plus-dwell trigger, cadence) remains DDR-003's. The distillation write's **checkpoint posture is explicitly un-decided**: whether writing a `derived` `ObservedPattern` into the Operational plane warrants a human-accountable checkpoint before entry is routed to the forthcoming **KG-entry-governance ADR** (see *Cross-References*, the boundary routing map), not asserted here. The distillation step (reading the SoR, generalizing patterns) writes `ObservedPattern` with `origin_mechanism: derived` / `derivation_class: distilled` and a `source_record_ref` to the external telemetry source; the process itself (cadence, generalization criteria, ownership) вҶ’ **DDR-003 / detection-promotion SDD**. Weakness/strength entry into the KG is **EA-gated** via the feedback loop (В§5), not auto-ingested. *This plane aligns to DDR-001's Operational-plane definition (durable distilled `ObservedPattern`s).*

### 2.4 Governance вҖ” the decision/audit graph (immutable append-only; references, not records)

**Decision supertype + subtypes.** A single shared decision *shape* with two subtype labels, so SDLC (software-development-lifecycle) gates and the feedback-loop promotion gate share structure without being conflated. **Subtype integrity:** every `Decision` carries **exactly one** subtype label вҖ” never bare, never both вҖ” enforced as a CI-only invariant (В§7; Neo4j cannot enforce label cardinality natively). The **approving-state set** (referenced by the promoted-origin invariant, В§7) is `{approved, approved_conditional}`.

- **`(:Governance:Decision)`** *(supertype shape вҖ” never instantiated bare; every decision node also carries a subtype label)* вҖ” `decision_id` (T1 PK) В· `decided_at` (T2) В· `approval_token_id` (T3) В· provenance (T1). Decided-by via `(Actor)-[:APPROVED]->(Decision)`. **The verdict is not a supertype property:** it rides the `DECIDED_ON {outcome вҲҲ approved | approved_conditional | rejected}` edge (В§3, В§5) вҖ” a property of *the decision's act on each target* вҖ” so a `GateDecision` carries one outcome and a batch `PromotionDecision` carries a per-candidate outcome on each edge, with no single-value supertype field to break on batches and no double-record.
- **`(:Governance:Decision:GateDecision)`** *(SDLC gates)* вҖ” the **enterprise SDLC gate on produced Solutions**: the external authority decides, and SOFIA mirrors the outcome as a captured reference. Adds `gate` (T2, indexed вҖ” SDLC gate discriminator; **placeholder `gate_0`/`gate_1`/`gate_2` (contested) вҶ’ reconcile to enterprise gate taxonomy**) В· the standard provenance group with **`origin_mechanism: ingested`** (the external-captured gate вҖ” the only grounded case; В§7 check #17 governs its `source_record_ref` obligation) В· **`source_record_ref`** `{source_system_class, record_id}` (the standard structured external reference, per В§1) В· `all_hard_constraints_passed` (T2) В· `rejection_reason` (T3, conditional). `DECIDED_ON {outcome}` вҶ’ **Solution** (В§5). The decided-in-SOFIA mechanics вҖ” a future human approval *issued* in SOFIA, or a SOFIA-issued approval вҖ” are routed by name to the **approval + governance-state-manager SDDs**; the origin-evolution path is the consolidated *gate-decision origin evolution* Named Gap (below).
- **`(:Governance:Decision:PromotionDecision)`** *(feedback-loop EA approval вҖ” SOFIA-issued)* вҖ” **`origin_mechanism: authored`** (the approving `Actor` is a human EA; SOFIA is the issuing authority) and therefore **no `source_record_ref`** вҖ” re-grounded on the В§1 provenance matrix's `authored вҶ’ primary` case (a SOFIA-recorded human decision), not an ad-hoc exception; В§7 check #17 exempts `authored`. `DECIDED_ON {outcome}` вҶ’ **CandidatePromotion** (В§5), **1:many** (a single batch decision may decide multiple candidates вҖ” the **per-candidate verdict lives on each `DECIDED_ON {outcome}` edge** вҖ” so one decision can approve some, reject others, and conditionally-approve others; `CandidatePromotion.status` separately carries the candidate's own *lifecycle* state, which legitimately diverges from a historical edge verdict). **Verdict precedence:** governance is append-only вҖ” a re-decision is a new `PromotionDecision` with a new `DECIDED_ON` edge, so a candidate may carry multiple `DECIDED_ON` edges with differing outcomes. The **governing** verdict is the outcome of the edge whose `PromotionDecision` has the **latest `decided_at`** (no `SUPERSEDES` edge type; re-decision is permitted). **Ties are structurally excluded:** the gateway enforces **per-candidate strict `decided_at` monotonicity** on `DECIDED_ON` writes вҖ” a new edge on a candidate must carry `decided_at` strictly greater than any existing edge's on that candidate вҖ” so "latest `decided_at`" is always well-defined and needs no tie-break (В§7 #15). В§7 conformance check #15 keys promotion authority off the *governing* approving edge, so a node materialized on a now-superseded approval is detected when the governing verdict has since become a rejection (its un-promotion remediation rides the **retraction shape**, В§5 / В§7 #21). Batch-eligibility *policy* вҶ’ DDR-003. **Retraction.** Un-promotion of a promoted KG fact is an **EA-gated reversing `CandidatePromotion` + `RETRACTS` edge** (В§5; В§7 #21). Its **upstream authority for un-promotion routes to the forthcoming KG-entry-governance ADR** (scope: mutation of enterprise ground truth generally вҖ” entry *and* un-promotion); ADR-001 В§2.5 authorizes promotion only and does not stretch to un-promotion. The *remedy boundary* вҖ” supersession (the default, when wrong-but-replaceable; В§6 versioning already supports it) vs. retraction (the exception, when the fact should never have been promoted and has no correct replacement) вҖ” is **DDR-003 governance**, not authored here; DDR-002 owns only the schema shape. A **post-materialization re-decision of an executed retraction** (the governing verdict on a terminal-`promoted` retraction later flipping вҖ” the retraction-side analogue of #15's flip case) is likewise remedy-boundary territory routed to DDR-003: the schema records the re-decision append-only under В§2.4's verdict precedence; the un-retraction remedy вҖ” and a standing detection analogue of #15 for executed retractions (#21 traces to *an* approving decision, not the *governing* one) вҖ” is not authored here.

- **`(:Governance:Actor)`** вҖ” `actor_id` (T1 PK) В· `actor_type` (T2, indexed вҖ” `human` / `system`) В· `name` (T3) В· provenance (T1)
- **`(:Governance:Role)`** вҖ” `role_id` (T1 PK) В· `name` (T3) В· provenance (T1)
- **`(:Governance:Attestation)`** вҖ” `attestation_id` (T1 PK) В· `result` (T2, indexed вҖ” pass/fail) В· `attested_at` (T2) В· provenance (T1, `origin_mechanism: authored` вҖ” SOFIA-registered) *(closes obligationвҶ”satisfaction, В§3)*
- **`(:Governance:Condition)`** *(applicability predicate for `approved_conditional` promotions)* вҖ” `condition_id` (T1 PK) В· `predicate` (T2 вҖ” structured, evaluable predicate over solution/Catalog properties; pattern-aligned with `rule_definition`, carrying a declared `dependency_manifest` вҖ” declared-introspectable, consumed at retrieval-time evaluation, В§7 #19; no edge-backing obligation, В§7 #10) В· `created_at` (T2) В· provenance (T1, `origin_mechanism: authored` вҖ” the same issuing act as its `PromotionDecision`). Linked from its `PromotionDecision` (`HAS_CONDITION`) and governs the conditionally-promoted node's applicability **by traversal** (not duplicated onto the node, beyond the `applicability_state` marker, В§5). The knowledge is promoted *with* this condition (not held pre-promotion). A `conditional` node is admitted to a consuming context only if that context **satisfies its `Condition` predicate** (single-condition, В§7 check #19; evaluated at retrieval; knowledge-service / solutioning SDDs), which is what lets the same knowledge be reused across qualifying solutions. **Multi-condition вҶ’ named DDR-003 gap (see *Named Gaps*):** if a fact is conditionally promoted more than once it is structurally **one node reached by multiple `HAS_CONDITION` paths** (forced by `(business_key, version)` uniqueness); the **multi-condition consumption semantics** (disjunctive vs. otherwise) and the **condition-set lifecycle** (activation/retirement/`status`; accrete vs. supersede) are a named gap вҖ” DDR-002 adds **no** `Condition.status` field now, resolving them when a real multi-condition case arises. Condition vocabulary вҶ’ DDR-003.

*Confidence-derivation basis:* **`flat_base`** (basis 2), plane-level вҖ” immutable append-only authoritative record; staleness is governed by re-decision/lifecycle, not decay (В§2 preamble; DDR-004).

*Key postures:* for `GateDecision` in the current build, the external entity is the system of record and the node is a **captured reference**, not SOFIA's authoritative record (`source_record_ref` carries the external reference; `origin_mechanism: ingested`). For `PromotionDecision`, SOFIA *is* the issuing authority over the feedback loop, so there is no external record to point at (`origin_mechanism: authored`, per the В§1 matrix) вҖ” the de-conflation keeps the SDLC-gate semantics from leaking onto the promotion gate and vice-versa. **`approved_conditional` on `GateDecision`** is a **faithful mirror** of a real enterprise gate outcome ("approved with conditions"); it does **not** constitute В§5 selection (only `approved` does, В§5), and its gate conditions are **not** the promotion `Condition` machinery (В§2.4/В§5) вҖ” the external record holds them, reachable via `source_record_ref`, and reusing the promotion predicate would re-introduce the exact gateвҶ”promotion leakage this de-conflation prevents. **Entry-path rule (Governance-plane types):** a **mirrored** external decision enters `origin_mechanism: ingested` + `source_record_ref`; a **SOFIA-issued or SOFIA-registered** decision enters `origin_mechanism: authored` with no `source_record_ref` (`PromotionDecision`, `Condition`, and вҖ” thinner inference вҖ” `Attestation`). `Actor` is the general decider identity вҖ” human, SOFIA, or another external system вҖ” each with a structural home; **`Actor`/`Role` sourcing** (deployment-shaped) is routed to the **governance-state-manager SDD**, bounded by this rule. The per-action **disposition-event stream is excluded** (transient вҶ’ orchestration/observability SoR). `Actor`/`Role` are governance-participating identities, **not** an IAM mirror. Immutable append-only: a re-decision is a new node.

## 3. KG relationships вҖ” edge grammar + catalog

**Edge grammar:**
- **Direction & naming** вҖ” `SCREAMING_SNAKE` verb phrases in the natural semantic direction; Neo4j traverses both ways, so direction is for readability, not access. One edge per relationship вҖ” no redundant inverses.
- **Edge properties** вҖ” attributes belonging to the *relationship*, not either node.
- **Edge provenance** вҖ” asserted-fact edges (ingested, e.g. `APPROVED_OPTION_FOR`) carry provenance (`origin_mechanism`, В§1); structural edges do not.
- **Re-bind discipline** вҖ” each edge *type* declares a supersession behavior, **governed by the point-in-time principle**: edges that record a *point-in-time fact* (a solution's use of a version, evidence citations, decision references) are **`rebind:pinned`** вҖ” they never re-point, because re-pointing would falsify the historical record; edges expressing a standing *current* pointer (current-recommended, selection-preference) are **`rebind:current`** вҖ” they re-point to the new version on supersession so the hot path stays current. Because every potentially-unbounded edge is pinned by correctness necessity, the `rebind:current` re-point set is **bounded-degree by construction**; the residual вҖ” any `rebind:current` type that proved unbounded вҖ” would fall back to identity-indirection for that type (not adopted now). Declared per edge type in the catalog below.
- **Cardinality** вҖ” not constrainable in Neo4j schema вҶ’ documented invariant + conformance check.
- **Temporal edges** вҖ” relationships whose *validity* is time-bound carry `effective_from`/`effective_to` + `status`; structural edges do not.
- **Confidence** вҖ” confidence lives **on the surface whose certainty it describes**: *edge* confidence = certainty of an observed relationship (Environment as-running edges; the Operational `OBSERVED_IN` per-target certainty); *node* confidence = reliability of a derived/reasoned thing (`ObservedPattern` lesson reliability, `CapabilityCostEstimate`, `ReasoningProgress`, `Evidence`). KG *structural* edges are authoritative by default (no confidence). Canonical definition + per-surface composition rule in В§4.

**Edge catalog (five core-plane sub-graphs + Cost/Extension):**

*Selection (Catalog) вҖ” `rebind:current`:* `(Pattern)-[:REQUIRES_CAPABILITY {required, tier_conditional, tier_threshold}]->(Capability)` В· `(Technology)-[:APPROVED_OPTION_FOR {conditional, justification}]->(Capability)` *(temporal)* В· `(Pattern)-[:PREFERRED_OVER]->(Pattern)` В· `(Technology)-[:REPLACED_BY]->(Technology)` *(temporal)* В· `(IacTemplate)-[:IMPLEMENTS]->(Pattern)`

*As-running (Environment, confidence-bearing):* `(DeployedService)-[:RUNS {confidence}]->(Technology)` В· `(DeployedService)-[:DEPLOYED_IN {confidence}]->(DeploymentEnvironment)` В· `(DeployedService)-[:REALIZES {confidence}]->(Capability)` В· `(ConfigurationItem)-[:PART_OF {confidence}]->(DeployedService)`

*Track-record (Operational):* `(ObservedPattern)-[:OBSERVED_IN {confidence}]->(Technology | Pattern | Capability | DeploymentEnvironment)` *(edge `confidence` = per-target observation certainty, distinct from the node's lesson-reliability `confidence` вҖ” composed at rollup)*

*Governance-of-knowledge (Standards):* `(Standard)-[:DEFINES]->(PolicyRule)` В· `(Standard)-[:PRESCRIBES]->(Technology | Pattern)` *(Process target deferred)* В· `(PolicyRule)-[:MAPS_TO]->(ComplianceControl)` В· `(PolicyRule)-[:MANDATES]->(Technology)` В· `(Pattern | Technology | Capability)-[:GOVERNED_BY]->(PolicyRule)` *(the stored, traversable record of **entity-triggered** applicability вҖ” any solution using this entity inherits this rule. Condition-triggered applicability is computed from `rule_definition`; the two combine in the obligation-closure model below.)*

*Decision/audit (Governance):* `(Actor)-[:HAS_ROLE]->(Role)` *(temporal)* В· `(Actor)-[:APPROVED {role, approved_at}]->(Decision)` *(targets either subtype via the supertype)* В· `(GateDecision)-[:REVIEWED {hash}]->(Artifact)` В· `(Attestation)-[:SATISFIES]->(PolicyRule)` В· `(Attestation)-[:BY]->(Actor)` В· `(Attestation)-[:EVIDENCED_BY]->(Artifact)`. *(`(Attestation)-[:FOR_PROCESS]->(Process)` deferred entirely.)* *Pointer (defined at the keystone, not duplicated here):* the `GateDecision` verdict edge `(GateDecision)-[:DECIDED_ON {outcome}]->(Solution)` is defined at the Solution keystone (В§5).

*Cost (Extension):* `(CapabilityCostEstimate)-[:FOR_CAPABILITY]->(Capability)` *(cardinality 1)* В· `(CapabilityCostEstimate)-[:HAS_COST_FACTOR]->(CostFactor)` В· `(CostFactor)-[:FOR_TECHNOLOGY]->(Technology)` В· `(CapabilityCostEstimate)-[:PRICED_BY]->(RateCard)` *(`rebind:pinned`)*

*Solution-centric and promotion edges are deferred to the keystone (В§5).*

**ObligationвҶ”satisfaction model (hybrid).** Standards hold the *obligation*; Governance holds the *attestation*; the solution sits in the middle. The **validator computes the obligation set** вҖ” *entity-triggered* obligations reached by traversing `GOVERNED_BY`/`MANDATES` from the solution's `USES`/`FOLLOWS` entities, **plus** *condition-triggered* obligations from evaluating `rule_definition` conditions against the solution вҖ” then joins each obligation to its satisfaction: validator-structural (prohibitions, structural requirements) or attestation-evidenced (`Attestation`). The **closure is validator logic, not a single graph traversal** (the graph stores the associations, the rule logic, and the attestations). A missing satisfaction surfaces as a compliance gap (an RG risk inference), not silence. Closure logic + manifest consumption вҶ’ constraint-validator SDD.

It carries **no KG plane label** (not ground truth) and is a `derived` RG-region node. *Materialized **at promotion** (В§5, В§6), so build-before-`Evidence`-expiry holds by construction.* The internal structuring is fixed above (index-aligned per-`Evidence` entries keyed by `evidence_id`); the affordance warrants **no CI invariant of its own** вҖ” frozen-layer reachability is exactly what В§7 #20 already guarantees (existence, completeness, at-promotion binding), and a second invariant would re-verify #20 under another name. What remains deferred is the **reverse-lookup index / query-pattern optimization**, pending the retention policy DDR-003 owns (see *Named Gaps*).

**Reasoner attribution + the LLM-advisory invariant.** `reasoner_category` realizes ADR-001 В§2.2's per-artifact source attribution by category on `ReasoningProgress`; `authoritative` realizes its authoritative flag as an **explicit, indexed boolean** (the ADR-001 В§5.2 read-discipline filter surface) вҖ” denormalized-for-introspection and kept honest by the fixed categoryвҶ’flag mapping (В§7 check #23), **not** derived at read (the `dependency_manifest` species). The **LLM-advisory invariant:** LLM-rendered content rides an **authored** artifact as **non-authoritative content properties** вҖ” never as an authored node of its own вҖ” and the hosting artifact's `reasoner_category` / `authoritative` values govern how reads treat it; the rich payload itself stays T3/T4, SDD-shaped.

**Confidence вҖ” canonical definition.** Confidence is a `[0.0, 1.0]` scalar living **on the surface whose certainty it describes**: **node** confidence = reliability of a derived/reasoned thing вҖ” on `Evidence` *source authority Г— freshness* (authoritative Catalog above aging Environment, decayed by the cited source's age вҖ” its own `observed_at` вҖ” as of the capture instant, `Evidence.observed_at`), on `ObservedPattern` the *lesson reliability*, on `CapabilityCostEstimate` the estimate reliability, on `ReasoningProgress` the *rolled-up conclusion*; **edge** confidence = certainty of an observed relationship вҖ” Environment as-running edges, and `OBSERVED_IN` *per-target observation certainty*. Thus **Environment = edge-only; Operational = node (lesson reliability) + edge (per-target certainty)**, the two composed at rollup. **Inheritance/rollup rule:** `Evidence.confidence` inherits from the `SOURCED_FROM` KG node's authority class at snapshot time; `ReasoningProgress.confidence` is a (SDD-defined) rollup over its `SUPPORTED_BY` Evidence вҖ” DDR-002 fixes that the rollup exists and is **upper-bounded by its strongest supporting evidence path**: the **ceiling = `max` of the supporting `Evidence.confidence`** (a conclusion is no more confident than its strongest evidence path), and **`weight` affects aggregation only *below* the ceiling, never the ceiling itself**; the **zero-evidence case** (a conclusion with no `SUPPORTED_BY` Evidence) is left to the SDD that defines the rollup function. The rollup function itself вҶ’ SDD (В§7 check #24 enforces the ceiling; if the SDD's function redefines path strength, the comparator amends with it).

**`conclusion_type` вҶ” correction-surface mapping.** The mapping onto В§5's seven correction-surfaces (technology, pattern, capability, integration, risk, compliance, cost) is **two-axis, not a flat valuesвҶ’surfaces arrow**. Four surfaces map **directly** to a `conclusion_type`: technology вҶ’ `TechnologySelection`, pattern вҶ’ `PatternMatch`, risk вҶ’ `RiskSignal`, compliance вҶ’ `ComplianceEvaluation`. **Capability** homes on **`GapConclusion`** per DDR-001's gap definition (gap = a required capability with no resolving technology вҖ” DDR-001 В§Two-Graph Model, cross-cutting KG invariants). **Integration and cost** are the stated **absorbed variants** вҖ” `TechnologySelection` / `GapConclusion` variants rather than distinct conclusion types. **`OverrideFlag` is flavor-carrying** (the Refine flavor, В§5), **orthogonal to the surface axis** вҖ” it rides any surface rather than naming one. The mapping aligns with DDR-001's (coarser) signal/action classes. *(The enum remains contested pending the spike-generalization pass.)*

*Write authority (**ADR-002 В§2.6** invariant):* ASA authors `ReasoningProgress`; AOE owns `ReasoningSession` lifecycle only; writes route via knowledge-service. Mechanization deferred (В§7).
*RG-internal edges:* `(ReasoningSession)-[:CONTAINS]->(ReasoningProgress)` В· `(ReasoningProgress)-[:SUPPORTED_BY]->(Evidence)` В· `(ReasoningProgress)-[:REJECTED]->(RejectedAlternative)` В· `(ReasoningProgress)-[:LED_TO]->(ReasoningProgress)`.
*Point-in-time fidelity:* `fact_summary` + `source_node_version` make the audit trail immune to later KG drift.

**RG provenance posture.** Provenance is present across the RG, but expressed by node kind:
- `ReasoningSession` and `ReasoningProgress` are *authored reasoning state* вҶ’ they carry the provenance group (provenance = the authoring session/agent, ASA per В§2.6).
- `RejectedAlternative` is **authored content** вҶ’ it carries the provenance group (`origin_mechanism: authored`), because DDR-001 В§Reasoning Graph frames it as ASA-authored and the *primary feedback-loop input*; the highest-leverage promotion signal must not carry the weakest provenance.
- `Evidence` is the **sole** *surrogate-only* RG type вҖ” the sole derived RG type that omits the group; its provenance is **structural** вҖ” it pins `source_node_version`, stamps the capture instant (`observed_at`), and links `SOURCED_FROM` the authoritative KG node it snapshots (В§5), written as an **atomic capture-unit** (`Evidence` + `SOURCED_FROM` in one gateway transaction, В§7) so the surrogate is **sound by gateway contract** (CI-enforced via В§7 check #14, exposure named вҖ” not a DB guarantee). This structural attribution вҖ” source recoverable by `SOURCED_FROM` + version-pin traversal, authoritativeness inherited-confidence-weighted from the cited KG node's authority class вҖ” **satisfies ADR-001 В§2.2's source-attribution intent for this type**, without carrying `reasoner_category` / `authoritative` (those are the attribution surface for the *conclusion* type, `ReasoningProgress`).
- `ProvenanceSummary` is a **`derived` RG type that carries the group** (`origin_mechanism: derived`) вҖ” it is **not** surrogate-only; it is a materialized derivation whose own origin is recorded, distinct from the `Evidence` structural surrogate it preserves. It therefore joins the В§7 existence-constraint scope.

So the provenance *existence constraint* (В§7) scopes to **KG nodes + the three authored RG types (`ReasoningSession`, `ReasoningProgress`, `RejectedAlternative`) + `ProvenanceSummary` (derived, group-carrying) + the Artifact family**; `Evidence` remains the **sole** surrogate-only type. **The surrogate's enforceability gap is named, not hidden:** the cardinality invariants that *make* the `Evidence` surrogate sound вҖ” including the **atomic capture-unit** вҖ” are CI-only (В§7); Neo4j cannot express them as constraints, so they are documented and mechanized ahead of the gateway (В§7).

**Accountability boundary.** RG content is captured *judgment*, not held to the KG determinism invariant. The EA gate (`PromotionDecision`) is the **accountable-consolidation control** вҖ” it converts a judgment-derived proposal into an *accountable, ratified, authoritative* KG fact (per ADR-001 В§2.5's "EA-gated consolidation into encoded logic"). It restores **accountability and authoritativeness, not determinism**: determinism is a separate, never-lost property of the gap/override *derivation* (a structural function of graph facts вҖ” DDR-001 В§Two-Graph Model, cross-cutting KG invariants), independent of how any fact entered. Promoted facts are therefore **in-scope** for that derivation by design (the encoding-density-growth feature вҖ” a promoted override becomes sanctioned and future deterministic reasoning reads it) and remain **distinguishable** via `origin_mechanism: promoted`. A promoted-origin KG node must still trace to an **approving** `PromotionDecision` (В§7) вҖ” that ties it to an *accountable* decision. The *policy* of how the derivation weights promoted-vs-ingested facts, and the *health* of judgment-consolidation over time вҶ’ DDR-003 / reasoning-machinery.

## 5. Cross-graph linkage + the feedback loop

**Ownership line вҖ” what DDR-002 owns vs. cites in this section.** DDR-002 **owns** the *schema contract*: the `(:Artifact:Solution)` and `CandidatePromotion` node shapes, the cross-graph and promotion edge grammar, and the *structural* invariants (append-only terminal status, provenance-chain traceability). DDR-002 **cites, and does not re-author:** the promotion *mechanics / data-path* вҶ’ DDR-001; the *diagnosis policy / EA approval criteria / thresholds / cadence* вҶ’ DDR-003; the *workflow* вҶ’ SDD. The Refine/Request-new/Correct-scope framing and the "EA-gate is a diagnosis" characterization below are stated for schema motivation only вҖ” their governance is DDR-003's, their data-path is DDR-001's.

**The Artifact family вҖ” a node-family within the two-graph store.** `(:Artifact вҖҰ)` is a **distinct node-family**, *not* a third graph or third citizen-class peer to the KG and RG: the store is the two-graph model (KG + RG, DDR-001), and Artifacts are nodes *within* it that bridge produced deliverables to the reasoning that made them and the catalog they draw on. It is **neither a KG plane nor RG** вҖ” the produced-deliverable family (solutions, BCDR plans, build sheets). It carries its own PK, its own provenance group (`origin_mechanism: authored`/`derived`), and its own uniqueness, and it carries **no KG plane label** (so plane-scoped ground-truth traversals skip produced artifacts). It joins KG and RG only by edges. This resolves the question of whether `Solution` is a Catalog node or an RG node: it is **neither** вҖ” it is an Artifact node-family member (DDR-001 В§Versioning & Temporal Model and В§Hybrid Persistence Model вҖ” solution dual-home).

**Evidence edges (RG вҶ’ KG):** `(Evidence)-[:SOURCED_FROM]->(KG node)` *(`rebind:pinned`; confidence inherits here вҖ” authoritative Catalog scores above aging Environment)* В· `(RejectedAlternative)-[:WOULD_HAVE_USED]->(KG node)` *(`rebind:pinned` вҖ” a point-in-time fact per В§3's re-bind discipline)*. *(`DREW_FROM`/`TRAVERSED` deferred вҖ” the considered-set is reconstructable from Evidence + RejectedAlternative.)*

**The Solution keystone:**
- **`(:Artifact:Solution)`** вҖ” `artifact_id` (T1 PK) В· `artifact_type` (T2 вҖ” `solution`) В· `version` (T1) В· `lifecycle_state` (T2, indexed вҖ” FSM (finite-state-machine): proposedвҶ’architectedвҶ’gatedвҶ’approvedвҶ’operational) В· `content_hash` (T2 вҖ” tamper-detection) В· `snapshot_ref` (T2 вҖ” dual-home pointer) В· `target_environment` (T2 вҖ” design-time intent, drawn from the `environment_class` controlled vocabulary) В· `created_at` (T2) В· provenance (T1, `origin_mechanism: authored`). `(:Artifact)` is the general produced-deliverable family (solutions, BCDR plans, build sheets).

*Bridge edges:* `(ReasoningSession)-[:PRODUCED]->(Solution)` *(**0..\*** вҖ” a session may produce multiple candidate options or none)* В· `(Solution)-[:USES]->(Technology)` В· `(Solution)-[:FOLLOWS]->(Pattern)` В· `(GateDecision)-[:DECIDED_ON {outcome}]->(Solution)` В· `(Solution)-[:HAS_ARTIFACT]->(Artifact)` *(Solution вҶ’ component sub-artifacts)*. `target_environment` is a **controlled-vocabulary string** (the `environment_class` value-set) resolved by string-match, **not** an FK (foreign-key)-edge to `DeploymentEnvironment`; the realized environment arrives later via `DeployedService`. Dual-home mechanics вҶ’ В§6.

**Selection among produced candidates.** When a session `PRODUCED`s multiple candidate `Solution`s, **selection is the approving `(GateDecision)-[:DECIDED_ON {outcome: approved}]->(Solution)` edge** plus `lifecycle_state` advancement (`gated вҶ’ approved`) вҖ” **`approved` is the sole selection-constituting outcome** (a mirrored `approved_conditional` records a real enterprise gate outcome faithfully but does **not** constitute selection, В§2.4) вҖ” **no `selected` flag** (a redundant boolean would re-create the divergence the edge-borne verdict avoids). Before gating there is no recorded selection (selection is a governance act); the ASA's pre-gate *primary-candidate* pick is solutioning-runtime state вҶ’ solutioning SDD. Non-selected candidates remain `proposed`/`architected` and are retained as the explored option-set. **Boundary вҖ” not-selected `Solution` vs `RejectedAlternative`:** a not-selected `Solution` is an **artifact-level** complete candidate deliverable that wasn't gated; a `RejectedAlternative` (В§4) is a **decision-step-level** discarded conclusion *within* the reasoning trail. They are different granularities and not interchangeable; "why a candidate wasn't chosen" is reasoning reachable via the gate decision вҖ” no new edge.

**CandidatePromotion + feedback loop** вҖ” *schema contract owned here; mechanics cited to DDR-001 per the ownership line above:*

- **`(:Reasoning:CandidatePromotion)`** вҖ” `candidate_id` (T1 PK) В· **`proposal_kind`** (T2, indexed вҖ” enum `{promotion, retraction}`; the explicit **direction discriminator**, so property-filtered reads no longer have to traverse to the `RETRACTS` edge to tell forward promotions from reversals) В· `promotion_type` (T2, indexed вҖ” the promotion's *subject/kind*, distinct from direction) В· `proposed_change` (T3 вҖ” opaque) В· `requested_item` (T3) + `requested_item_kind` (T2 вҖ” technology/pattern/capability/integration) + `target_ref` (T3) *(for request-new gaps)* В· `basis_strength` (T2 вҖ” *contested*) В· `confidence` (T2) В· `status` (T2, indexed вҖ” proposed / under_review / approved / **approved_conditional** / rejected / promoted) *(the candidate's **lifecycle** state; the decision's per-candidate verdict rides the `DECIDED_ON {outcome}` edge вҖ” and the two legitimately diverge)* В· `created_at` (T2) В· provenance (T1). **Append-only**; terminal `status` (`promoted` / `rejected {reason}`) is the durable evaluation outcome вҖ” declines are explainable and gaps don't re-surface. A single `PromotionDecision` may decide this candidate as part of a **batch** (1:many `DECIDED_ON {outcome}`); `approved_conditional` carries an applicability `Condition` (В§2.4). **Reversing (retraction) variant:** a retraction is the **same `CandidatePromotion` node carrying `proposal_kind: retraction`** plus a `RETRACTS` edge (В§Promotion edges) вҖ” it goes through the **same EA gate** (`DECIDED_ON` by an approving `PromotionDecision`) as a forward promotion, so un-promotion is accountable, not a bare delete (В§7 #21, #25). It reuses the **shared `status` lifecycle** вҖ” the FSM tracks **proposal execution**, read uniformly across both `proposal_kind`s: terminal `status: promoted` denotes the proposal's effect was **materialized** (for a retraction: the un-promotion applied вҖ” the `RETRACTS` edge written and the target read-excluded вҖ” *not* that anything was forward-promoted); this is a deliberate economy, not an accident, and no new `status` value is added. The remedy-boundary *governance* (supersession vs. retraction) is DDR-003; the schema shape is owned here.

- **Provenance-survival guarantee.** A **durable terminal-`promoted`** node's provenance must remain **recoverable after its originating `Evidence` is retained-out**, preserved as **frozen traversable structure** (not an opaque blob вҖ” post-expiry audit and derivation-nature recovery stay a traversal, not a parse), and **materialized before** the `Evidence` it summarizes is removed. **Span (the originating-`Evidence` set to freeze):** the **closure over the promotion's `PROPOSED_FROM` targets** to their Evidence-reaching edges вҖ” an `Evidence` target contributes itself; a `ReasoningProgress` target contributes its `SUPPORTED_BY` `Evidence` set; an `ObservedPattern` target contributes nothing to freeze (a durable, live-traversable KG node); a `RejectedAlternative` target contributes no `Evidence` (`WOULD_HAVE_USED` is not Evidence-mediated). The two Evidence-reaching paths are `PROPOSED_FROM` (to an `Evidence` directly) and `PROPOSED_FROM` вҲҳ `SUPPORTED_BY` (via a `ReasoningProgress`); the **gateway computes this closure in the materialization transaction** (future Evidence-reaching edges amend the span вҖ” contract maintenance, not a hedge). This is the schema-relevant guarantee DDR-002 commits (grounded in DDR-001 В§Versioning & Temporal Model вҖ” summary-on-evidence-expiry; the provenance chain's expiry-survival, below, leans on it). The form is a dedicated **`(:Reasoning:ProvenanceSummary)`** node (В§4, `origin_mechanism: derived`) вҖ” **not** a frozen subgraph вҖ” that denormalizes the expiring `Evidence` content (the index-aligned, `evidence_id`-keyed `frozen_*` entry arrays, В§4) as a retention-boundary snapshot and stays bound to the durable promotion record by `(:ProvenanceSummary)-[:MATERIALIZES_PROVENANCE_OF]->(CandidatePromotion)`. Materialization is **at-promotion**, so the build-before-`Evidence`-expiry ordering holds **by construction** (it is an invariant of the promotion act, В§7 #20 вҖ” not a sweep that races retention). The residual gap is the *reverse-lookup index / query-pattern optimization* over `ProvenanceSummary`, to finalize with the retention policy and the first real instance (see *Named Gaps*; the affordance warrants no CI invariant of its own вҖ” В§4). Framed as a *retention-boundary snapshot*, not hot-path denormalization (В§1).

**`applicability_state` marker вҖ” on promoted KG nodes.** Every node materialized via `PROMOTES_TO_KNOWLEDGE` carries `applicability_state` (T2, indexed вҖ” `unconditional` (default) / `conditional`), **distinct from** the version-lifecycle `status` (a node can be the active version *and* `conditional`). It is the structural key the В§7 read-discipline invariant filters on: active ground-truth traversals admit a `conditional` node only where the consuming context satisfies its `Condition` (В§2.4, В§7 check #19). A `conditional` node is retained and fully traceable вҖ” never hard-deleted. **Per-version + supersession carry-forward:** `applicability_state` is **per-version**, established by that version's own promotion decision. Supersession (В§6) must **preserve** an EA-set conditional scope вҖ” a successor to a `conditional` node is **not** consumed `unconditional` absent an explicit EA `PromotionDecision` re-scoping it (a silent default-to-`unconditional` would over-admit past an EA-set `Condition`, a failure В§7 check #19 cannot catch because the successor would no longer be marked `conditional`). DDR-002 commits this integrity expectation and mechanizes it as **В§7 check #22** (conditional-scope carry-forward on supersession, predecessor-keyed, safety-critical tier); the carry-forward *mechanism* on supersession (including any cross-origin case) вҶ’ **knowledge-service SDD / conformance-mechanization**. **Two-surface applicability:** applicability is constrained on two distinct surfaces вҖ” **Catalog eligibility** fields (`tier_applicability[]`, `approved_data_classifications[]`) on **ingested** `Technology` (В§2.1), and `applicability_state: conditional` + `Condition` on **promoted** knowledge вҖ” and **both bind**. Their composition into a single consumption decision ("may I use this here?") is the consuming SDD's responsibility вҶ’ **knowledge-service / solutioning SDDs**; DDR-002 names the two surfaces, not a unification mechanism.

*Promotion edges:* `(CandidatePromotion)-[:PROPOSED_FROM]->(ReasoningProgress | Evidence | ObservedPattern | RejectedAlternative)` В· `(PromotionDecision)-[:DECIDED_ON {outcome}]->(CandidatePromotion)` *(the feedback-loop EA gate вҖ” distinct subtype; **1:many** for batch decisions with per-candidate `outcome`)* В· `(PromotionDecision)-[:HAS_CONDITION]->(Condition)` *(for `approved_conditional`)* В· `(CandidatePromotion)-[:PROMOTES_TO_KNOWLEDGE]->(KG node)` *(materialized node stamped `origin_mechanism: promoted` + `applicability_state`)* В· `(ProvenanceSummary)-[:MATERIALIZES_PROVENANCE_OF]->(CandidatePromotion)` *(`rebind:pinned` вҖ” the at-promotion provenance snapshot's binding to its durable promotion record, В§7 #20)* В· `(CandidatePromotion)-[:RETRACTS]->(KG node)` *(`rebind:pinned` вҖ” the reversing-CandidatePromotion's un-promotion target; EA-gated, В§7 #21)*.

*Structural & read-discipline invariants:*
- **Proposal-visibility (dissolved to DDR-001 + gateway).** `CandidatePromotion` must not appear in ground-truth synthesis traversals until EA-approved. This is **owned by DDR-001** as the proposal-visibility invariant + conformance check #4 (`SOFIA never self-modifies the KG`; promotions excluded from ground-truth traversal until approved). DDR-002 supplies the *structural aid* вҖ” `CandidatePromotion` carries no KG plane label, so plane-scoped traversals skip it вҖ” but names honestly that full enforcement is **gateway-enforced read-discipline** (the sole-owner knowledge-service scopes ground-truth reads), **not** a pure structural guarantee. Lands in the CI-only set (В§7); cites DDR-001 check #4, no DDR-001 amendment needed.
- **Provenance chain.** A promoted node traces back through `PROMOTES_TO_KNOWLEDGE` вҶ’ `CandidatePromotion` вҶ’ `PROPOSED_FROM` вҶ’ `ReasoningProgress`/`Evidence`/`RejectedAlternative` вҶ’ `SOURCED_FROM` вҶ’ original facts; `origin_mechanism: promoted` makes it distinguishable from `ingested` (DDR-001 check #6). Its pre-promotion *derivation nature* is recovered by this same traversal to the source, not denormalized. **Expiry survival:** when the originating `Evidence` expires, the chain is preserved by the **provenance-survival guarantee** (above) вҖ” frozen traversable structure via the `ProvenanceSummary` node; the *retrieval affordance* over it is the residual named gap. **Approving-decision invariant:** a promoted node must trace, via `PROMOTES_TO_KNOWLEDGE` вҶҗ `CandidatePromotion` вҶҗ the **governing** approving `DECIDED_ON` edge вҖ” the edge whose `PromotionDecision` has the **latest `decided_at`**, with `outcome вҲҲ {approved, approved_conditional}` вҖ” to a `PromotionDecision` (CI-only, В§7 #15). Keying off the *governing* edge (not any approving edge) means a node whose governing verdict has since flipped to `rejected` is detected even if a stale earlier `approved` edge exists; its un-promotion remediation is the **retraction shape** (the EA-gated reversing `CandidatePromotion` + `RETRACTS` edge, В§7 #21; remedy-boundary governance вҶ’ DDR-003).
- **Retracted-node read-discipline.** A KG node carrying an inbound `RETRACTS` edge from an **EA-approved** reversing `CandidatePromotion` is excluded from ground-truth synthesis traversals вҖ” **gateway-enforced read-discipline keyed on the EA-approved `RETRACTS` edge**, parallel to В§7 checks #9 (proposal-visibility) and #19 (conditional-consumption); no denormalized `retracted` status marker (the edge is the structural fact). The retracted node is **retained and fully traceable** (durable rationale on the audit trail) вҖ” never hard-deleted.

**Generalized correction mechanism** *(schema motivation; governance вҶ’ DDR-003).* Every `conclusion_type` is a human-correction surface (technology, pattern, capability, integration, risk, compliance, cost вҖ” reconciled to the `conclusion_type` enum in В§4), in three flavors: **Refine** (override to an existing option вҶ’ context-scoped, evidence-backed preference signal on the selection edge, no pairwise cycles; the *runtime preference-landing* вҶ’ solutioning SDD), **Request-new** (gap вҶ’ human evaluation/onboarding; never auto-created), **Correct-scope** (false risk / mis-scoped rule вҶ’ adjust existing ground truth). The capture machinery (`overridden_by_human`, `human_accepted`, `GapConclusion`, `RejectedAlternative`, `CandidatePromotion`, EA-gate, `PROMOTES_TO_KNOWLEDGE`) is general across surfaces. The "override is a signal the EA *diagnoses*" framing is governance characterization owned by DDR-003, not authored here.

## 6. Versioning & temporal model

**Per-posture spectrum** (each plane fit to its nature): Catalog/Standards/RateCard **version** (supersession), Environment **ages** (`observed_at`), Operational **distills** (status-managed), Governance **accretes** (immutable append-only), RG **pins** (point-in-time), Solutions/Artifacts **dual-home**.

**Supersession вҖ” Option A (scoped to versioned-ground-truth types: Catalog, Standards, RateCard, CostFactor, PlaneDefinition).** A node carries `version` + `effective_from`/`effective_to` + `status` + `superseded_by`; superseding creates a new retained node and marks the old superseded. **Atomicity:** supersession is a **single atomic transaction** вҖ” create new version node, mark old `superseded`, and re-point the `rebind:current` edges to the new node вҖ” so a traversal never observes a half-superseded state with two active versions. `rebind:pinned` audit/evidence/consumption edges are untouched (they pin to their specific version node). **The re-point is O(degree) but bounded by construction:** the point-in-time principle (В§3) forces every potentially-unbounded edge (solution-consumption, evidence, decision-reference) to be `rebind:pinned`, so the `rebind:current` set is limited to standing current-pointer edges (low-degree); the assumption is stated, and the residual вҖ” any `rebind:current` type that proved unbounded вҖ” falls back to identity-indirection for that type (not adopted now). The edge re-pointing is the **gateway's** responsibility (knowledge-service, sole writer вҖ” consistent with DDR-001's gateway pattern; cited, not re-authored). Never-delete via retained versions; as-of via effective-dating. Chosen over a blanket identity+version-node pattern to keep the selection-graph hot path hop-free (versioning is rare; traversal is constant).

**Version-pinning.** `Evidence.source_node_version` + `CapabilityCostEstimate`'s pinned basis (`rate_card_version_ref` / `cost_factor_version_ref` / `capability_version_ref`) reference specific retained version nodes (`rebind:pinned`); with `Evidence.fact_summary` that yields point-in-time fidelity. The immutable as-of cost record is the cost-plane instance of this point-in-time discipline.

**Durable promotion records.** A terminal-`promoted` `CandidatePromotion` is **expiry-exempt** (durable); the **provenance-survival guarantee** (В§5) holds that when bounded RG retention expires the originating `Evidence`, the promoted node's traceability is preserved as **frozen traversable structure** (not a blob), materialized before the `Evidence` is removed, keeping post-expiry audit graph-native (schematizing DDR-001's summary-on-evidence-expiry вҖ” В§Versioning & Temporal Model). The form is a dedicated `(:Reasoning:ProvenanceSummary)` node (В§4), materialized **at promotion** via `MATERIALIZES_PROVENANCE_OF` so build-before-expiry holds by construction (В§7 #20). The **retention policy** (the `Evidence`-expiry windows the retention classes set) вҶ’ DDR-003, and the **retrieval index / query-pattern optimization** over `ProvenanceSummary` вҶ’ knowledge-service SDD (named gap вҖ” see *Named Gaps*).

**Dual-home (Artifact/Solution).** The graph node holds metadata, edges, `content_hash`, `snapshot_ref`; immutable content lives in the external snapshot store (Firestore, routed to DDR-001); the hash is tamper-detection (snapshot hash must match `content_hash`). A new version is a new node + new snapshot; the old pair is retained.

**Uniqueness under versioning.** PKs unique on `(business_key, version)`; "exactly one active version per business_key" is a documented invariant + conformance check.

## 7. Constraints, indexes & conformance

**DB-enforced (Neo4j Enterprise) вҖ” generating rules:**
- **Uniqueness** on every PK; `(business_key, version)` for the versioned-ground-truth types.
- **Existence constraints** on the provenance group (`origin_mechanism`, `recorded_at`) on every **KG** node, the **three authored RG types** (`ReasoningSession`, `ReasoningProgress`, `RejectedAlternative`), the **derived `ProvenanceSummary`** (`origin_mechanism: derived`), and the **Artifact family** (see В§4 for the RG-provenance posture) вҖ” plus each node's T1 required props. (The Enterprise *edition* committed in ADR-002 buys existence-constraint capability.)
- **Indexes** on the flagged T2 traversal/filter properties only (lean вҖ” no speculative indexing); plane secondary-labels are inherently indexed. *The index set is **provisional**, revisitable once the SDDs surface real query patterns.* **Reverse cross-graph lookup:** `SOURCED_FROM` is stored RGвҶ’KG, so the reverse direction (KG-node вҶ’ citing-`Evidence`, "what reasoning cited this fact?") needs an index/affordance вҖ” named here; the concrete index lands with the retrieval patterns in the knowledge-service SDD.

**Conformance-checked invariant set вҖ” CI-only (Neo4j cannot express вҶ’ documented + CI; enforcement posture below).** Neo4j existence/uniqueness constraints cannot express these; they are honestly named as CI-only with a stated exposure window (below):

1. **RG-provenance edge invariants (safety-critical tier, sequenced ahead of the gateway):** every `(:Reasoning:Evidence)` carries a **mandatory** `SOURCED_FROM` edge to a provenance-bearing KG node; every `(:Reasoning:RejectedAlternative)` is reached via **exactly one** parent `REJECTED` edge вҖ” justified on **per-conclusion** grounds: a `RejectedAlternative` carries per-conclusion rejection rationale (`rejection_reason`/`score_delta`/`human_accepted`), so it *is* the rejection-by-one-conclusion; the same option rejected by two conclusions is two nodes the feedback loop aggregates by their shared `WOULD_HAVE_USED` target (better for recurrence detection than a shared node). `Evidence` **not** sourced from a provenance-bearing KG node is **prohibited** (`Evidence` is KG-sourced by definition and check #14 enforces it). These cardinality invariants are what make the В§4 structural provenance-surrogate sound.
2. **no vector properties.**
3. **relationship cardinality** (e.g. `FOR_CAPABILITY` cardinality 1).
4. **one active version per business_key.**
5. **aggregate-derivation** (`CapabilityCostEstimate.aggregate_cost` derived-at-creation-then-frozen, never hand-set).
6. **schema-on-write** (extension nodes validate vs. `PlaneDefinition`; edges bounded to `attaches_to`).
7. **ADR-002 В§2.6 write-authority** (ASA authors `ReasoningProgress`; AOE session-lifecycle only).
8. **never-delete** (versioned types supersede; atomic re-bind bounded by the point-in-time rebind assignment).
9. **proposal-visibility / read-discipline:** `CandidatePromotion` excluded from ground-truth synthesis traversal until EA-approved вҖ” gateway-enforced read-discipline + structural label-skip aid; defers to DDR-001 check #4.
10. **`rule_definition`вҶ”edge sync:** every Catalog entity-type in a **`PolicyRule`'s** declared `dependency_manifest` appears as a `GOVERNED_BY`/`MANDATES` target вҖ” edge-backing is scoped to `PolicyRule` manifests, the case В§3's edge grammar defines. A **`Condition`'s** `dependency_manifest` is **declared-introspectable only**: it is consumed by retrieval-time predicate evaluation (#19) and carries no edge-backing obligation (no `GOVERNED_BY`/`MANDATES` grammar exists for `Condition`, В§3). **Caveat:** this enforces *declared-but-wired*, not *declared-complete* against the opaque logic; the completeness check (a rule reads only manifest-declared entities) is validator behavior вҶ’ constraint-validator SDD.

## Layer of Abstraction & Substrate Stability

This DDR operates at the **data-substrate / schema layer** вҖ” it fixes the node/relationship/constraint/index contract over the data architecture DDR-001 establishes, gating the downstream service SDDs and cited by DDR-003 (governance). Schema churn is expensive downstream, so the completeness posture is deliberately reversibility-scoped: T1/T2 are locked now; T3/T4 stay schema-on-write and SDD-level.

Amendments to the locked T1/T2 contract вҖ” node keys, the provenance two-axis model, edge grammar and `rebind` discipline, the supersession/temporal model, or the CI-only invariant set вҖ” carry blast radius into those consumers and require coordinated review. The named gaps (above) are the designated additive-amendment surface; re-instating one is an additive amendment, not a breaking change. The one-way dependency direction to DDR-001 is explicit: DDR-002 depends on DDR-001; DDR-001 does not depend on DDR-002's schema.
